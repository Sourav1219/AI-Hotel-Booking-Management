Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI booking assistant work?
Our AI booking assistant analyzes your preferences, travel history, and search patterns to provide personalized hotel recommendations. It learns from your interactions to improve suggestions over time, helping you find accommodations that perfectly match your needs and budget.
Can I cancel my reservation?
Yes, most reservations can be cancelled up to 24-48 hours before check-in, depending on the hotel's policy. You can manage your bookings in the "My Bookings" section of your account. Some special rates may have different cancellation policies, which will be clearly indicated during booking.
Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and payment information. We comply with PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) requirements and never store your full credit card details on our servers.
How can I get the best deals on hotels?
Our AI assistant constantly monitors prices and promotions to find the best deals. Booking in advance, traveling in off-peak seasons, and being flexible with your dates often results in better prices. You can also enable price alerts for specific destinations to be notified of special offers.
What if I need to modify my reservation?
You can modify most reservations through the "My Bookings" section. Changes to dates, number of guests, or room type are usually possible, subject to availability and potential price adjustments. Some changes may be subject to the hotel's modification policy.
Can I book multiple rooms at once?
Yes, you can book multiple rooms in a single reservation. During the booking process, you'll have the option to select the number of rooms needed. You can specify different room types and guest information for each room if required.
